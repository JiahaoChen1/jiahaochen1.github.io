Jiahao Chen（陈嘉浩）

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at Tsinghua University, working with Prof. Xiangyang Ji. I received my Ph.D. from Renmin University of China under the supervision of Prof. Bing Su. Before that, I earned my M.S. and B.S. degrees from Harbin Institute of Technology, where I was advised by Prof. Rui Wu during my master's study. My research interests lie in reliable and adaptable machine learning under realistic settings, with a particular focus on long-tailed learning, representation learning, and AI4Science.

我目前是清华大学博士后研究员，合作导师为 季向阳教授。 我在中国人民大学获得博士学位，博士导师为 苏冰副教授。 此前，我在哈尔滨工业大学先后获得硕士和学士学位，硕士导师为 吴锐副教授。 我的研究聚焦于真实场景下可靠且具有适应性的机器学习，主要关注长尾学习、表征学习与科学智能。
