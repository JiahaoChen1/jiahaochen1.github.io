Jiahao Chen

I'm a 4th-year PhD student at Renmin University of China, where I am advised by Prof. Bing Su. I received my bachelor's and master's degrees from Harbin Institute of Technology. My research focuses on developing more reliable and adaptable machine learning models for realistic scenarios, with particular emphasis on addressing challenges in long-tailed distributions and representation learning. I am passionate about bridging the gap between theoretical advances and practical applications in computer vision and AI4Science domains.
